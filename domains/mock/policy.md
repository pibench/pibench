# Mock Domain Policy

This is a mock policy for testing purposes.

## Section 1 — General Rules

All requests must be logged before processing.
Requests from unauthorized users must be denied.
Sensitive information must not be disclosed to third parties.

## Section 2 — Escalation

If the request involves an amount exceeding $10,000, escalate to a supervisor.
If the policy does not cover the situation, escalate rather than guessing.
